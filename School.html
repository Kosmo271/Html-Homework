I go to Engelska Skolan in Linköping, Sweden (The English School)

I have been going there since 4th grade (2022) and have a lot of memories from this school and have met many amazing friends here!

IESL (The International English School of Linköping) is a school which was founded trough IES which is a multi national organization for education. There are many IES schools all over the world in countyies such as Germany, England, Italy, ECT.

Internationella Engelska Skolan (IESL) in Linköping is a bilingual F–9 school that blends Swedish and English education in a safe, structured, and academically ambitious environment. Founded in 2003, the school is nestled in the heart of Linköping, surrounded by the historic seminarieträdgården park, which adds a touch of serenity to the learning atmosphere.

At IESL, up to half of the instruction is delivered in English by qualified teachers from English-speaking countries such as Canada, the UK, and the US.
